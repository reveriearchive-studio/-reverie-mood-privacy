You can delete your data at any time:

Reverie Mood is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Reverie Mood is not a medical app. It's designed for personal wellness tracking only. If you're experiencing mental health concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.
